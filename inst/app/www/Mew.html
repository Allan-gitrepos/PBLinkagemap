PBlinkagemap

Map Your QTLs

Welcome to PBlinkagemap! This web app enables you to easily create linkage maps and identify associated quantitative trait loci (QTLs) from your genomic and phenotypic datasets.

Just import your data including chromosome, markers, map distances and trait scores - PBlinkagemap will handle the QTL analysis and mapping. Interactive results make it simple to explore marker-trait associations.
